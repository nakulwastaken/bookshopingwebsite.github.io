The Hobbit and the Lord of the Rings



The Hobbit and The Lord of The Rings Trilogy are the bestselling
fantasy novel series written by, J. R. R. Tolkien.

Readers from all
around the world have been enchanted by the sheer magnitude of the
stories written by Tolkien and are mesmerized by the magic in his words.
These books have been widely successful, having sold more than 150 million
copies worldwide and been translated into over 50 languages. The books have
also been adapted into six successful Hollywood films that have been acclaimed
both critically and financially. This boxed set is the complete collection of
J. R. R. Tolkien’s middle earth epic fantasy series that begins with The Hobbit
and ends with The Return of The King.


This new boxed set was published to commemorate the 75th anniversary
of the time when The Hobbit was first published. This latest design
provides the reader with an exciting chance to rediscover the epic world
of Tolkien’s middle earth. The publishers have ensured that the quality
of the Boxed set is credible enough to please everyone. The books have been
deemed as classic right from the moment they were first published.

About the Author

J. R. R. Tolkien is a name that has become a synonym with the
fantasy segment. His works, The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings
trilogy have been lauded with success. The boxed set of the Middle-Earth
fantasy series was published by HarperCollins Publications in the year 2011
and is available in both paperback and hardcover.

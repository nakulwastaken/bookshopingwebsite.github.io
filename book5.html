Less (Winner of the Pulitzer Prize): A Novel


A struggling novelist travels the world to avoid an awkward wedding
in this hilarious Pulitzer Prize-winning novel full of "arresting lyricism
and beauty" (The New York Times Book Review).

 Longlisted for the Andrew Carnegie Medal for Excellence,

Who says you can't run away from your problems?
You are a failed novelist about to turn fifty.
A wedding invitation arrives in the mail:
your boyfriend of the past nine years is engaged
to someone else. You can't say yes--it would be too awkward
--and you can't say no--it would look like defeat. On your desk
are a series of invitations to half-baked literary events around
the world.
